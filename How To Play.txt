-Scooby Food Fight is a competitive game played by two players.
-Player 1 controls Shaggy and player 2 controls Scooby.
-The controls for each player can be viewed and changed in the settings menu.
-Each player must collect as many falling food items as possible within 2 minutes.
-Enemies will spawn and stun the player for a few seconds if they collide with one of them.
-Each food item has a different score value as shown below:

Apple: 10
Orange: 10
Carrot: 20
Cookies: 25
Pancake: 30
Donut: 30
Melon: 35
Ice Cream: 40
Cake: 50
